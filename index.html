SAGIT | What we are about

To create a wholesome theatre and performance education through theatrical materials revolving around social issues pertinent to humanity. In the process, SAGIT aims to enhance, stimulate as well as celebrate understanding of ourselves and others through the shared experience of live theatre by producing new and classic plays spiced with innovative reflections and interpretations relevant to the world in which we live.
